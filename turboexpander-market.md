<p><ul><li>Radial Turboexpander</li><li>Axial Turboexpander</li><li>Radial-Axial Turboexpander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turboexpanders are devices used to convert the energy from expanding gases into mechanical work. They are used in various industries such as oil and gas, liquefied natural gas (LNG), and petrochemicals. Radial turboexpanders have a radial flow path, with the gas entering and exiting radially. Axial turboexpanders, on the other hand, have an axial flow path. Radial-axial turboexpanders combine both radial and axial flow paths. Each type has its own advantages and applications, depending on the specific industry and requirements.</p></p>
