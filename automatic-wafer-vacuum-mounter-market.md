<p><p>The automatic wafer vacuum mounter market is divided into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. This type of mounter is suitable for high volume production and provides efficient and precise results. On the other hand, the semi-automatic market includes mounters that require some manual operations alongside automated functions.
