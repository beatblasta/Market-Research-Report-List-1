<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The weather barrier market can be categorized into three types based on their level of permeability: impermeable, semi-permeable, and permeable. Impermeable weather barriers are designed to completely block the passage of moisture, air, and other elements. Semi-permeable barriers allow some level of moisture and air transfer through them while still providing protection. Permeable barriers, on the other hand, allow for significant moisture and air exchange while still offering some level of protection against external elements. These different types cater to varying weather conditions and building needs.</p></p>
