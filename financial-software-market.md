<p><p>Financial software market can be broadly categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software refers to solutions that are installed and operated on a company's own servers and infrastructure. It offers greater control and customization options but is often more costly and requires regular maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It provides flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making it popular among businesses. Additionally, it eliminates the need for maintenance as updates are automatically applied by the provider.</p></p>
