<p><p>Measuring current transformers are used to measure electrical current while protective current transformers are designed to provide protection against overcurrent situations. Measuring current transformers are commonly used in power systems for metering and billing purposes. Protective current transformers are used in conjunction with protective relays to detect overcurrents and trigger protective actions to prevent damage to electrical equipment. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and safe operation of electrical systems.</p></p>
