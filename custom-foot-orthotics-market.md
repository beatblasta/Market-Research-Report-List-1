<p><p>The custom foot orthotics market consists of three main types of orthosis: rigid, soft, and semi-rigid orthosis. Rigid orthosis is made of a hard material and provides maximum support for people with severe foot conditions. Soft orthosis is made of cushioning materials and provides comfort and relief for individuals with sensitive feet. Semi-rigid orthosis combines elements of both rigidity and flexibility, offering moderate support for people with varying foot conditions. These different types cater to the specific needs of individuals, providing targeted treatment for a wide range of foot ailments.</p></p>
