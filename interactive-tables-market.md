<p><ul><li>Liquid Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Light-Emitting Diode (LED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interactive tables market consists of two main types: Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) and Light-Emitting Diode (LED). LCD technology uses a layer of liquid crystals between two transparent electrodes to display images or videos. It offers good image quality, wide viewing angles, and low power consumption. On the other hand, LED technology uses tiny light-emitting diodes to create an image or video display. LED tables offer high brightness, vibrant colors, and better energy efficiency compared to LCD tables. Both types are commonly used in interactive tables for various applications.</p></p>
