<p><p>The Automobile Clutch Material market is divided into three main types based on their mechanism: Friction Clutch, Centrifugal Clutch, and Semi Centrifugal Clutch. Friction Clutches are the most common type and work by transmitting power through friction between two surfaces. Centrifugal Clutches operate using centrifugal force to engage the clutch at high speeds. Semi Centrifugal Clutches are a combination of both friction and centrifugal mechanisms, providing a balance between performance and durability in various automotive applications.</p></p>
