<p><p>The plate magnetic separator market consists of two main types: wet magnetic separators and dry magnetic separators. Wet magnetic separators use water as the medium to separate magnetic materials from non-magnetic ones. They are commonly used in mineral processing industries. On the other hand, dry magnetic separators use air as the medium to separate magnetic materials. They are mostly used in recycling, ceramics, and mining industries. Both types of separators are designed to remove unwanted magnetic materials from bulk products to enhance product purity and quality.</p></p>
