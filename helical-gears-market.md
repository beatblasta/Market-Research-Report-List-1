<p><p>In the helical gears market, left hand and right hand refer to the type of helix angle of the gear teeth. Left hand helical gears have their teeth sloping towards the left, while right hand helical gears have their teeth sloping towards the right. This determines the direction of rotation of the gears and is crucial for their compatibility when used in gear systems. The choice of left hand or right hand gears depends on the specific application and the overall gear system design requirements.</p></p>
