<p><p>Single-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps and Two-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps are two types of rotary piston pumps used in various industries. Single-stage pumps have a single rotor and stator arrangement, providing a relatively lower level of vacuum. On the other hand, two-stage pumps use two rotor-stator configurations, resulting in a higher vacuum level. These pumps are commonly utilized in applications requiring the removal of air or gas from a sealed chamber, such as in vacuum packaging, laboratory experiments, or industrial processes.</p></p>
