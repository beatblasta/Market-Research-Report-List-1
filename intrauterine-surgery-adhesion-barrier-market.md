<p><p>The intrauterine surgery adhesion barrier market includes three main types: film formulation, gel formulation, and liquid formulation. Film formulation involves using a thin layer of a substance that adheres to the affected area and helps prevent adhesion formation. Gel formulation involves a thicker substance that can be applied directly to the surgical site and provides a barrier between tissues. Liquid formulation refers to a more fluid substance that can be sprayed or applied through irrigation to prevent adhesion formation during surgery. These different formulations cater to diverse surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
