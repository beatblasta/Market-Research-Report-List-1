<p><p>The semiconductor biosensor market includes various types of sensors utilizing different biological components. Enzyme-based biosensors use enzymes to detect and measure analytes in biological samples. Nucleic acid-based sensors detect specific DNA or RNA sequences. Lectin-based sensors use lectins to identify and bind to specific carbohydrates. Antibody-based sensors utilize antibodies to recognize and detect specific antigens. Cell-based sensors use living cells as sensing elements, while organ-based sensors use organs or tissues for detection purposes. These different types of biosensors provide diverse solutions for detecting and analyzing biological samples.</p></p>
