<p><p>Near field communication (NFC) market can be divided into two types: non-auxiliary products and auxiliary products market. Non-auxiliary products refer to NFC-enabled devices that are standalone products, such as smartphones, tablets, and smartwatches. These devices have built-in NFC technology, allowing users to easily make contactless payments, transfer data, and access services. On the other hand, the auxiliary products market includes NFC-enabled accessories or add-ons that can be attached to existing non-NFC devices, like NFC tags, stickers, or cases. These auxiliary products enable traditional devices to have NFC capabilities and expand their functionalities.</p></p>
