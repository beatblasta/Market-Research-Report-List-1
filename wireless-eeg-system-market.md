<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wireless EEG System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WiFi</li><li>Bluetooth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless EEG system market includes various types such as WiFi and Bluetooth. WiFi-based systems use the wireless local area network to transmit EEG data from the electrodes to a receiver device, which can then be connected to a computer or mobile device for analysis. Bluetooth-based systems utilize the Bluetooth technology to establish a short-range wireless connection between the electrodes and a receiver device. Both WiFi and Bluetooth systems provide convenient and wire-free solutions for EEG data acquisition, allowing for improved mobility and ease of use in various applications.</p></p>
