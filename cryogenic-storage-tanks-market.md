<p><p>Cryogenic storage tanks are specialized containers used to hold and transport liquefied gases such as oxygen, nitrogen, argon, and LNG (liquefied natural gas). They are widely used in various industries including chemical, pharmaceutical, and healthcare.</p><p>The future outlook for the cryogenic storage tanks market appears positive. The increasing demand for liquefied gases, especially LNG, for various applications such as power generation, transportation, and industrial processes is driving the growth of this market.
