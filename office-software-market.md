<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Office software market types can be categorized into on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise software requires installation and maintenance on individual computers or servers within a company's infrastructure. It provides greater control and customization options but requires more resources and technical expertise. Conversely, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs as companies pay for usage. However, data security and dependability on internet connectivity are important factors to consider in the cloud-based market.</p></p>
