<p><p>The Plastic Inspection Chamber market comprises two main types: Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chamber and Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chamber. Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chambers are manufactured through a rotational molding process, where plastic is heated and rotated inside a mold to form the desired shape. These chambers are known for their durability, strength, and resistance to corrosion. On the other hand, Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chambers are constructed by welding plastic pipes together to create a chamber structure. They offer flexibility in design and installation, making them suitable for complex piping systems.</p></p>
