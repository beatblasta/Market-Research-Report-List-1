<p><p>Prefilled IV flush syringes are commonly available in two main types: Saline IV flush syringes and Heparin IV flush syringes. Saline IV flush syringes contain a sterile solution of saline that is used to flush intravenous lines to prevent blockages and maintain patency. On the other hand, Heparin IV flush syringes contain the anticoagulant medication heparin, which helps prevent blood clots from forming in the IV line. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and functionality of intravenous access in healthcare settings.</p></p>
