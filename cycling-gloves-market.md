<p><p>Cycling gloves come in two main market types: partial finger and full finger. Partial finger gloves, also known as fingerless or short finger gloves, have openings for the fingers, leaving them exposed. These gloves provide a good grip and allow for better dexterity, suitable for warm weather or intense rides. On the other hand, full finger gloves cover all the fingers entirely, offering protection from weather conditions and potential injuries. They are commonly used in colder climates or during off-road cycling where more protection is needed.</p></p>
