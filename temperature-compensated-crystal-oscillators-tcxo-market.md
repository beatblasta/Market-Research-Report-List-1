<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (TCXO) are devices that are used to generate highly stable and accurate clock signals in electronic systems. TCXOs are available in two market types: SMD Shape and PIN Shape.</p><p>SMD Shape TCXOs are designed in a surface mount package, making them suitable for applications where space is limited, such as mobile devices and wearables. These compact TCXOs provide excellent frequency stability and can be easily soldered onto PCBs.</p><p>PIN Shape TCXOs, on the other hand, come in a through-hole package with leads that are inserted into holes on the PCB. They are commonly used in industrial applications that require high precision and stability, such as aerospace and defense systems.</p><p>Both SMD Shape and PIN Shape TCXOs offer temperature compensation mechanisms to counteract the changes in crystal frequency caused by temperature variations, ensuring reliable and accurate timing in electronic devices.</p></p>
