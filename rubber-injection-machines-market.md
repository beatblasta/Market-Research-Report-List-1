<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber injection machines are used in the manufacturing industry to inject rubber materials into molds to create various products. There are two main types of these machines: vertical type and horizontal type. </p><p>Vertical type machines have a vertical injection unit and a vertical clamping system, making them suitable for products with a complex shape or deep cavity. They are also preferred for their high precision and easy operation.</p><p>On the other hand, horizontal type machines have a horizontal injection unit and a horizontal clamping system. These machines are generally used for larger products or mass production, as they offer higher production efficiency and stability.</p></p>
