<p><ul><li>New Floors</li><li>Retrofit Floors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polished concrete market comprises two main types: new floors and retrofit floors. New floors refer to the installation of polished concrete in newly constructed buildings or construction projects. It involves the entire process of preparing the surface, grinding, and polishing to achieve the desired level of smoothness and shine. Retrofit floors, on the other hand, involve the transformation of existing concrete floors into polished ones. This process requires repairing any damages or imperfections on the surface before proceeding with the grinding and polishing stages. Both types cater to different needs, whether it's for new construction or renovating existing spaces.</p></p>
