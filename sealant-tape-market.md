<p><p>Sealant tapes are categorized into four main types based on their density: low density, standard density, medium density, and high density. Low density sealant tapes are lightweight and flexible, suitable for sealing small gaps and joints. Standard density tapes offer a balance between flexibility and strength, ideal for general sealing applications. Medium density tapes provide added durability and resistance to compression for more demanding sealing tasks. High density tapes are the most rigid and durable, ensuring a tight seal in high-pressure or high-temperature environments.</p></p>
