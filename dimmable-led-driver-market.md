<p><strong>The Dimmable LED Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Width Modulation (PWM)</li><li>Analogue Dimming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dimmable LED Driver Market consists of two main types: Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) and Analogue Dimming. </p><p>PWM is a digital dimming technique that rapidly switches the LED on and off to achieve different brightness levels. It provides precise and flicker-free dimming, making it suitable for applications requiring high accuracy.</p><p>Analogue Dimming, on the other hand, employs a continuous voltage or current signal to adjust the LED's brightness. It offers a smooth dimming experience but may experience flickering at low light levels. This type is commonly used in residential and commercial lighting applications.</p></p>
