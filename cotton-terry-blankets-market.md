<p><p>The cotton terry blankets market consists of two types: woven and knitted. Woven blankets are made by interlacing cotton yarns in a perpendicular pattern, creating a durable and tightly woven fabric. These blankets offer great breathability and warmth. On the other hand, knitted blankets are made by interlocking loops of cotton yarn. They are softer, stretchier, and have a more relaxed drape compared to woven blankets. Knitted blankets are known for their comfort and coziness. Both types cater to different preferences and purposes within the cotton terry blankets market.</p></p>
