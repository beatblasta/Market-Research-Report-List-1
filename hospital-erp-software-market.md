<p><strong>The Hospital ERP Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On Premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hospital ERP Software Market is categorized into two types: On Premise ERP and Cloud-based ERP. On Premise ERP refers to a software system that is installed and operated on the hospital's own infrastructure. It requires upfront investment in hardware and maintenance but provides complete control and customization. On the other hand, Cloud-based ERP is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, cost-effectiveness, and requires no infrastructure maintenance, making it a popular choice for hospitals looking for flexibility and convenience in their software solutions.</p></p>
