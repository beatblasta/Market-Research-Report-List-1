The second type comprises chairs with above 3 degrees of freedom adjustment, offering a more advanced range of customization options, such as adjustable armrests, lumbar support, and headrest. These chairs cater to individuals who require a higher level of comfort and support to reduce the risk of musculoskeletal issues caused by prolonged sitting.</p></p>
