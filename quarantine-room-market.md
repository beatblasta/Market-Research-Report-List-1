<p><ul><li>Temporary Quarantine Room</li><li>Fixed Quarantine Room</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quarantine room market can be classified into two types: temporary quarantine rooms and fixed quarantine rooms. Temporary quarantine rooms are designed for short-term use, typically in emergency situations or during outbreaks. They are portable and can be quickly set up and dismantled as needed. On the other hand, fixed quarantine rooms are permanent installations in healthcare facilities or airports. They are equipped with specialized ventilation systems and other necessary features to safely isolate individuals with contagious diseases for an extended period.</p></p>
