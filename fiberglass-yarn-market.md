<p><ul><li>Fiber Yarn</li><li>Fiber Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass yarn market includes fiber yarn and fiber roving types. Fiber yarn is a continuous strand of twisted or spun fibers used for weaving or knitting. It is popular for its strength, flexibility, and resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, fiber roving consists of loosely assembled fibers used in the production of composite materials. It is favored for its lightweight properties and ability to be easily molded into various shapes. Both types play a crucial role in industries like construction and automotive for reinforcing structures and components.</p></p>
