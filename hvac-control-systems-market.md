<p><strong>The HVAC Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensors</li><li>Controlled Devices</li><li>Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC Control Systems market consists of three main types: Sensors, Controlled Devices, and Controllers. Sensors are devices that measure temperature, humidity, pressure, and other environmental variables and provide this information to the control system. Controlled Devices are components like motors, valves, and dampers that adjust the heating, cooling, and ventilation in response to the control system's commands. Controllers are the central units that receive data from sensors, process it, and send commands to controlled devices to maintain the desired environmental conditions. These three types work together to ensure efficient and effective HVAC control.</p></p>
