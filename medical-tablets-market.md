<p><p>The medical tablets market is classified into two types: daily use and operation use. Daily use tablets are designed for general healthcare purposes, such as patient documentation, accessing electronic healthcare records, and communication with medical staff. These tablets are user-friendly and equipped with features like long battery life and wireless connectivity. On the other hand, operation use tablets are specialized for surgical or diagnostic applications, typically used by medical professionals in operating rooms or during medical procedures. These tablets are built with sterile and rugged designs to meet the strict requirements of the medical environment.</p></p>
