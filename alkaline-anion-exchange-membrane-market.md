<p><strong>The Alkaline Anion Exchange Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perfluorocarboxylic acid membrane (Rf-COOH)</li><li>Perfluorosulfonic acid membrane (Rf-SO3H)</li><li>Perfluorosulfonic acid/acid composite membrane (Rf-SO3H / Rf-COOH)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alkaline anion exchange membranes are generally categorized into three types: perfluorocarboxylic acid membrane (Rf-COOH), perfluorosulfonic acid membrane (Rf-SO3H), and perfluorosulfonic acid/acid composite membrane (Rf-SO3H / Rf-COOH). The Rf-COOH membranes have carboxylic acid functional groups, while Rf-SO3H membranes contain sulfonic acid groups. The composite membranes combine both sulfonic acid and carboxylic acid functionalities. These different types of membranes offer varying degrees of alkaline stability, ion conductivity, and chemical resistance, serving different applications in the market.</p></p>
