<p><p>Diaphragm buffer tanks are essential components in HVAC systems, providing a storage space for excess water volume and reducing pressure fluctuations. There are two main types of diaphragm buffer tanks: chilled water buffer tanks used in cooling systems and hot water buffer tanks used in heating systems. Chilled water buffer tanks help maintain optimal temperatures in air conditioning units, while hot water buffer tanks ensure consistent hot water supply in heating systems. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining system efficiency and preventing damage from pressure surges.</p></p>
