On the other hand, rotary motors generate rotational motion and are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics for applications such as robotics, machine tools, and power generation. These motors eliminate the need for traditional transmission systems, leading to higher precision, increased efficiency, and reduced maintenance.</p></p>
