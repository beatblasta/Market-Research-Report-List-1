<p><p>High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) Power Supply Market includes two types of polarities - Positive Polarity and Negative Polarity. Positive Polarity in HVDC systems means that the voltage at the anode is higher than the voltage at the cathode, while Negative Polarity refers to the opposite situation where the voltage at the cathode is higher than the voltage at the anode. These polarities are important in determining the direction of current flow and are used in various applications such as long-distance power transmission and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
