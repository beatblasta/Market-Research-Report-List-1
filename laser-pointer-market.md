<p><p>Laser pointer market types consist of Class 2 products and Class 3R products. Class 2 products are those that emit visible laser beams with low power levels and pose a low risk of eye injury. These are commonly used in educational, business, and presentation settings. On the other hand, Class 3R products emit higher power levels of visible laser beams and carry a moderate risk of eye injury. They are typically used in environments where safety precautions can be implemented, such as industrial settings and certain entertainment applications.</p></p>
