<p><p>The dual tech occupancy sensors market includes two types of sensors: ceiling mount occupancy sensors and wall mount occupancy sensors. Ceiling mount occupancy sensors are installed on the ceiling and use a combination of ultrasonic and passive infrared technology to detect occupancy in a room. They are commonly used in large spaces and provide a wide detection range. On the other hand, wall mount occupancy sensors are installed on the wall and use the same combination of ultrasonic and passive infrared technology, but they offer a smaller detection range and are typically used in smaller rooms or hallways.</p></p>
