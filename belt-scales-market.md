<p><p>Belt scales are devices used in conveyor systems to measure the quantity of material being transported. There are three types of belt scales in the market: single-idler, two-idler, and multi-idler. Single-idler belt scales consist of one weighing idler, providing a cost-effective solution for simple applications. Two-idler scales use two weighing idlers to enhance accuracy and reduce belt loading. Multi-idler scales have multiple idlers to distribute the load evenly, offering the highest level of accuracy. Each type of belt scale offers varying levels of accuracy and cost, catering to different application requirements.</p></p>
