<p><p>The EMC antennas market caters to various applications, including EMC radiation test and EMC immunity test. In EMC radiation testing, antennas are used to measure and evaluate the electromagnetic radiation emitted by electronic devices, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. On the other hand, EMC immunity testing utilizes antennas to subject electronic devices to electromagnetic interference (EMI) in order to verify their immunity and performance in real-world scenarios. Both these applications play a crucial role in ensuring the electromagnetic compatibility of electronic devices to maintain their safe and reliable operation.</p></p>
