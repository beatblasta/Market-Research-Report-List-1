<p><p>Electric standers are assistive devices used by individuals with mobility impairments to help them achieve a standing position. There are two main market types for these devices: half-seated and upright. In the half-seated market, the stander allows the user to remain partially seated while still enabling them to stand up. This type of stander is suitable for individuals who have difficulty transferring from a fully seated to a standing position. On the other hand, the upright market refers to standers that provide full upright support, allowing users to stand completely. These standers are ideal for individuals who have the strength and ability to fully bear weight on their legs.</p></p>
