<p><p>Butt weld fittings market types include elbows, tees, reducers, caps, and others. Elbows are used to change the direction of the piping system. Tees allow for branching out of the pipeline into multiple directions. Reducers are used to connect pipes of different sizes. Caps are used to seal the ends of pipes. Other types of butt weld fittings include crosses, stub ends, and laterals, which are used in specific applications where there is a need to redirect or divide the flow of fluids. These fittings are essential components in various industries, such as oil and gas, chemical, and construction.</p></p>
