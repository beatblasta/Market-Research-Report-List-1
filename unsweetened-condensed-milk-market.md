<p><p>Unsweetened condensed milk comes in two main types: skimmed evaporated milk and whole evaporated milk. Skimmed evaporated milk is made by removing the fat content from fresh milk, resulting in a lower calorie and fat product. On the other hand, whole evaporated milk retains the full-fat content from fresh milk, providing a richer and creamier taste. These variations allow consumers to choose according to their dietary preferences and desired flavor intensity in recipes or as a stand-alone product.</p></p>
