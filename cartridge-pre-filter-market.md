<p><p>Cartridge pre-filter market comprises various types such as reflux filter, circulating filter, and others. Reflux filter technology involves the use of a special valve that allows the liquid to flow through the cartridge and then reverse the flow, effectively flushing out accumulated particles. Circulating filter, on the other hand, continuously circulates the liquid through the cartridge, preventing clogs and enhancing the filtration process. Other types encompass a range of different technologies and designs used for specific applications, offering unique benefits such as higher flow rates or better particle retention.</p></p>
