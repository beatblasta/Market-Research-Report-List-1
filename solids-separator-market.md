<p><p>Solids separator market types include centrifugal, magnetic, gravity, and other types. Centrifugal separators use centrifugal force to separate solid particles from liquids or gases. Magnetic separators use magnetic fields to attract and remove magnetic materials from the mixture. Gravity separators utilize the difference in specific gravity between solid and liquid components to achieve separation. Other types of separators may employ unique mechanisms or technologies to separate solids, such as sieving or filtration processes. These various market types cater to different applications and industries requiring effective solid separation solutions.</p></p>
