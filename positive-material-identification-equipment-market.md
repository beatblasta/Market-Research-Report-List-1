<p><strong>The Positive Material Identification Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positive Material Identification Equipment Market includes two main types: X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) and Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES). XRF technology uses X-rays to analyze the composition of materials, providing non-destructive and rapid results. It is commonly used in industries like manufacturing, mining, and jewelry. On the other hand, OES works by emitting light to identify the elemental composition of a material. It is often applied in metal analysis and quality control. Both technologies offer accurate and reliable material identification solutions, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
