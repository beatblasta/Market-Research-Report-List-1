<p><p>The antistatic wrist strap market comprises two main types: corded wrist straps and cordless wrist straps. Corded wrist straps consist of a strap that attaches to the user's wrist, with an attached cord that connects to a grounding point. This ensures static charge is safely conducted away from the user. Cordless wrist straps, on the other hand, provide the same function without the need for a cord. They utilize alternative methods like battery-powered modules to neutralize static charges and protect sensitive electronic components. These two types cater to the diverse needs of individuals working in different environments.</p></p>
