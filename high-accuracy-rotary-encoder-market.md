<p><p>The high accuracy rotary encoder market consists of two main types: incremental rotary encoders and absolute rotary encoders. Incremental rotary encoders generate a series of pulses in response to rotational movement, allowing for precise position tracking. They are commonly used in applications where relative position information is required. On the other hand, absolute rotary encoders provide unique digital codes for each position, ensuring accurate and continuous monitoring of rotational movement. They are often utilized in applications where absolute position information is essential. Both types of encoders play a crucial role in industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automation.</p></p>
