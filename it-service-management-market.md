<p><p>IT Service Management (ITSM) refers to the policies, processes, and procedures implemented by organizations to design, plan, deliver, operate, and control IT services they offer to their customers. Within the ITSM market are various types, including Configuration Management, IT Asset Management, and DBMS Management.</p><p>1. Configuration Management: Involves managing and documenting the hardware and software components of an organization's IT infrastructure, ensuring proper setup, tracking changes, and maintaining accurate records.</p><p>2. IT Asset Management: Focuses on managing an organization's IT assets throughout their lifecycle, from procurement to disposal, in order to optimize resource usage, track licenses, and ensure compliance.</p><p>3. DBMS Management: Refers to the management of the database management systems used by organizations for storing, organizing, and retrieving data efficiently, while ensuring data security, integrity, and availability.</p></p>
