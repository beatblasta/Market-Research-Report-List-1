<p><strong>The Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Infrared Detectors are divided into two main market types: Active Thermal Infrared Detectors and Passive Thermal Infrared Detectors. </p><p>Active Thermal Infrared Detectors emit and receive their own infrared radiation, allowing them to measure temperature and heat patterns accurately. They are commonly used in applications such as temperature monitoring in industrial processes and medical diagnostics.</p><p>Passive Thermal Infrared Detectors solely rely on detecting naturally emitted infrared radiation from objects or their surroundings. They are widely used in security systems, surveillance, and firefighting applications, as they are capable of detecting heat signatures emitted by objects or individuals.</p></p>
