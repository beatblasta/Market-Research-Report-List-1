<p><p>Line proportioners are used in various industrial applications to accurately mix and control the flow of different fluids. There are two common types available in the market: flange type and screw type. Flange type line proportioners are designed to be installed between two flanges in a pipeline, allowing for easy installation and maintenance. On the other hand, screw type line proportioners are installed directly into the pipeline using threaded connections. Both types offer reliable flow control in different industrial processes, ensuring precise fluid mixing and dispensing.</p></p>
