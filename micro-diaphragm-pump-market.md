<p><p>The micro diaphragm pump market comprises two main types: liquid micropumps and gas micropumps. Liquid micropumps are designed to handle and transport small amounts of liquid, typically used in medical devices, chemical analysis, and inkjet printers. Gas micropumps, on the other hand, are used for moving or dosing gas, commonly utilized in medical diagnostic equipment, environmental monitors, and gas analyzers. These pumps cater to various industries and applications, enabling precise and efficient fluid control in compact systems.</p></p>
