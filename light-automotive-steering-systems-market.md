<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light automotive steering systems come in three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering, Electronic Power Steering, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering. Hydraulic Power Steering uses fluid pressure to assist in steering, providing a traditional and reliable option. Electronic Power Steering relies on sensors and an electric motor to assist steering, offering improved efficiency and responsiveness. Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering combines elements of both hydraulic and electronic systems for a balanced approach. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
