<p><p>Hollow mills are cutting tools used to create or enlarge holes in materials. They come in different market types based on the number of teeth they have. The 3 teeth market includes hollow mills with three cutting edges, which are typically used for light cutting and finishing operations. The 4 teeth market comprises hollow mills with four cutting edges, commonly used for general cutting purposes. Meanwhile, the above 4 teeth market includes hollow mills with more than four teeth, often preferred for heavy cutting tasks or when a higher material removal rate is required.</p></p>
