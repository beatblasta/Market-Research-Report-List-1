<p><p>The Gas Burners market consists of two main types, Natural Gas and LPG (liquified petroleum gas). Both are widely used for cooking and heating purposes. Natural Gas burners are connected to a natural gas supply network while LPG burners use gas stored in tanks. Natural Gas is considered more environmentally friendly and cost-effective as it is directly supplied through pipelines. LPG, on the other hand, is a portable and versatile option for areas without access to natural gas pipelines.</p></p>
