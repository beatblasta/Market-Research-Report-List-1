<p><ul><li>Grid-connected Microgrid</li><li>Off-grid Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart microgrid solutions market can be classified into two types: grid-connected microgrid and off-grid microgrid. A grid-connected microgrid is a system that is connected to the main power grid and can operate either independently or in conjunction with the grid. It provides added reliability, stability, and resiliency to the existing grid infrastructure. On the other hand, an off-grid microgrid is a self-contained system that is not connected to the main power grid. It is usually deployed in remote areas where access to the grid is limited or unavailable, providing reliable and sustainable power solutions.</p></p>
