<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Radiant floor heating is a method of heating indoor spaces by warming the floors themselves. It can be categorized into two main types: electric and hydronic. Electric radiant floor heating utilizes electric cables or mats installed beneath the floor surface to generate heat. On the other hand, hydronic radiant floor heating involves using a network of pipes filled with heated water or other fluids beneath the floor. Both systems work by radiating heat from the floor upwards, resulting in a more comfortable and energy-efficient heating solution for homes and buildings.</p></p>
