<p><p>Cushion paper market consists of three types: single layer paper, double layer paper, and honeycomb paper. Single layer paper is made with one ply of paper, providing basic protection for fragile items. Double layer paper offers an extra layer of protection, making it suitable for heavier or more delicate products. Honeycomb paper, on the other hand, utilizes a honeycomb structure, which offers superior strength and cushioning for fragile or heavy items. These different types cater to varying needs and requirements in the packaging industry.</p></p>
