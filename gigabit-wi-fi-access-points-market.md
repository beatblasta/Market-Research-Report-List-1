<p><p>Gigabit Wi-Fi access points are available in different market types, including single band and double band options. Single band access points operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency only, offering a lower speed but better range. Double band access points, on the other hand, operate on both the 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing higher speeds but shorter range. These market types cater to different needs, with single band access points being suitable for larger coverage areas and double band access points better suited for high-speed applications in smaller areas.</p></p>
