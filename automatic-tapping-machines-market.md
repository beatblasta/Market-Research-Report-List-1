<p><p>Automatic tapping machines are widely used in various industries for repetitive tapping operations. Fully automatic tapping machines are designed to perform the entire tapping process without any manual intervention. These machines can automatically feed the workpiece, start the tapping process, and eject the finished product. On the other hand, semi-automatic tapping machines require some manual intervention in terms of feeding the workpiece and starting the tapping process, but they complete the tapping operation automatically. Both types of machines offer efficient and reliable tapping solutions based on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
