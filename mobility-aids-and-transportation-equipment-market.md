<p><p>Mobility aids and transportation equipment market consists of various types of products such as electrically powered wheelchairs, manual wheelchairs, walking aids, mobility scooters, stretchers, and stair lifts. Electrically powered wheelchairs are designed for individuals who lack mobility in their arms or have limited upper body strength. Manual wheelchairs are propelled by the user or a caregiver and are suitable for short distances. Walking aids like canes and walkers provide support and stability. Mobility scooters offer a convenient and efficient way for individuals with mobility issues to move around. Stretchers are used for transporting patients who are unable to walk, while stair lifts provide a safe and comfortable way for individuals with mobility issues to access different levels of a building.</p></p>
