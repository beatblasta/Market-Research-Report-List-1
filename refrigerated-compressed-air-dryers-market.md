<p><p>Refrigerated compressed air dryers are used to remove moisture from compressed air systems. Two main types of these dryers are cycling refrigerated air dryers and non-cycling refrigerated air dryers. Cycling refrigerated air dryers operate on a cycle basis, where the refrigeration system turns on and off according to air demand. Non-cycling refrigerated air dryers, on the other hand, constantly run regardless of air demand. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages and are suitable for different applications in industries where dry air is required.</p></p>
