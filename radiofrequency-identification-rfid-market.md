Moderate Frequency RFID is commonly used for tracking assets and inventory in retail and healthcare industries. High Frequency RFID is used for more advanced applications such as contactless payment systems and electronic passports. Each market type offers different benefits and capabilities depending on the specific use case.</p></p>
