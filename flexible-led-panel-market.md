<p><p>The flexible LED panel market includes two main types based on their composition materials. Fiberboard-based flexible LED panels are made from a lightweight fiberboard material, offering flexibility and durability. They are commonly used in applications that require a curved or bendable display surface. On the other hand, metal-based flexible LED panels are composed of metal materials, providing better heat dissipation and higher stiffness. They are often utilized in applications that prioritize heat resistance and rigidity, such as outdoor signage and architectural lighting.</p></p>
