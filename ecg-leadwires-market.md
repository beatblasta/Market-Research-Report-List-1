<p><p>ECG leadwires are a crucial component of electrocardiogram machines used in medical settings. There are three main types of leadwires: angled, J shape, and straight. Angled leadwires have a bend at the end, allowing for easier attachment to the patient's body. J shape leadwires are shaped like the letter J, providing stability and preventing accidental disconnection. Straight leadwires, as the name suggests, have a straight design and are commonly used for standard ECG monitoring. Each type of leadwire serves a specific purpose and offers different advantages depending on the medical situation.</p></p>
