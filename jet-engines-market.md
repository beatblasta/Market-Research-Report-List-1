<p><p>Jet Engines Market Types:</p><p>1. Turbojet Engine: A type of jet engine that uses the principle of jet propulsion by expelling hot exhaust gases to generate thrust. It is commonly used in military aircraft and supersonic jets.</p><p>2. Turbofan Engine: Also known as a fanjet, this type of jet engine generates thrust by combining the principle of both jet propulsion and bypass airflow. Turbofan engines are commonly used in commercial airliners due to their improved fuel efficiency and low noise levels.</p><p>3. Turboprop Engine: This type of jet engine utilizes a gas turbine to drive a propeller that generates thrust. Turboprop engines are commonly used in smaller regional aircraft and offer benefits such as increased fuel efficiency and quieter operation compared to turbojets.</p></p>
