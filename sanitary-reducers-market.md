<p><p>Sanitary reducers are devices used in piping systems to connect pipes of different sizes. They feature a larger diameter on one end and a smaller diameter on the other. These reducers help in maintaining the flow of fluids or gases smoothly by reducing the pipe size without causing any turbulence or pressure drops. These reducers are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and biotechnology, where maintaining hygiene and preventing contamination are of utmost importance.</p><p>The market for sanitary reducers is expected to witness significant growth over the forecast period. <p><p>Sanitary reducers are commonly used in plumbing systems to connect pipes of different sizes. There are two main types: sanitary eccentric reducers and sanitary concentric reducers. Sanitary eccentric reducers have an offset center, meaning that one end is larger than the other and they are used to join pipes of different sizes while maintaining fluid flow. Sanitary concentric reducers, on the other hand, have the same center and are used to connect pipes of different sizes while ensuring smooth fluid flow. Both types are essential in maintaining proper plumbing systems.</p></p>
