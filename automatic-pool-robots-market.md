<p><p>The automatic pool robots market offers three types of robots: crawler drive, wheel drive, and self-propelled (floating) robots. Crawler drive robots are designed to crawl along the floor and walls of the pool, utilizing tracks for movement. Wheel drive robots use wheels for navigation and are generally better suited for above-ground pools. On the other hand, self-propelled (floating) robots operate on the water surface, using propulsion systems. Each type of robot offers unique advantages and is designed for specific pool environments to efficiently clean and maintain the pool.</p></p>
