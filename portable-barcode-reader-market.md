<p><strong>The Portable Barcode Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Barcode Reader</li><li>Linear Imager Barcode Reader</li><li>2D Imager Barcode Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable barcode reader market can be categorized into three types: laser barcode readers, linear imager barcode readers, and 2D imager barcode readers. Laser barcode readers use a laser beam to scan barcodes, providing accurate and fast scanning capabilities. Linear imager barcode readers capture barcode images using CCD (Charge Coupled Device) technology, making them versatile and durable. On the other hand, 2D imager barcode readers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, offering superior scanning performance and advanced features like image capture and decoding of complex barcodes.</p></p>
