<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The household built-in cooktop market can be classified into two types: Upwind Style and Downwind Style. Upwind Style cooktops have the ventilation system installed on top of the cooktop, ensuring that smoke and odors are drawn upwards and away from the users. On the other hand, Downwind Style cooktops have the ventilation system installed beneath the cooktop, guiding smoke and odors downwards and out of the kitchen. These different styles provide options for households based on their individual preferences and space availability.</p></p>
