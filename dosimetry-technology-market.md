<p><p>Dosimetry technology is used to measure and monitor the amount of radiation exposure received by individuals in various settings. There are three types of dosimetry technologies: TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeter), OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence), and RPL (Radiophotoluminescent). TLD uses crystals that emit light when heated, providing a measure of radiation exposure. OSL uses aluminum oxide detectors that release stored energy when exposed to light, enabling the measurement of radiation exposure. RPL utilizes specialty materials that emit light in response to radiation, allowing for accurate dosimetry measurements. These technologies ensure the safety and well-being of individuals working in radiation-prone environments.</p></p>
