<p><strong>The Industrial Food Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Crew</li><li>Twin Crew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial food extruder market is categorized into two types: single crew and twin crew. A single crew extruder consists of one screw, which is responsible for mixing and compressing the food materials. It offers simplicity and easy operation. On the other hand, a twin crew extruder has two screws that work together to accomplish the mixing and compaction process. This type of extruder offers advantages like better uniformity, increased processing capacity, and improved product quality. The choice between the two types depends on specific manufacturing requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
