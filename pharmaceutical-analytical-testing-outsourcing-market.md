<p><p>Pharmaceutical analytical testing outsourcing market comprises various types including bioanalytical testing, method development and validation, stability testing, and others. Bioanalytical testing involves the analysis of biological samples to determine the concentration of drugs and their metabolites. Method development and validation involves the establishment of reliable and accurate analytical methods for testing pharmaceutical products. Stability testing assesses the product's integrity and shelf life under different conditions. The "others" category includes various tests such as impurities testing, dissolution testing, and microbiological testing, among others. Companies outsource these services to specialized contract research organizations (CROs) for cost-effectiveness and expertise.</p></p>
