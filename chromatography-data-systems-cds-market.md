<p><p>Chromatography Data Systems (CDS) market can be categorized into three types: on-premise, cloud-based, and remotely hosted. On-premise systems involve installing and maintaining the software on the user's own servers and infrastructure, providing complete control and security but requiring more resources. Cloud-based systems store and process data on remote servers, accessible via the internet, offering scalability and flexibility. Remotely hosted solutions combine the benefits of on-premise and cloud-based systems, where the software is hosted by a third-party provider, reducing hardware costs and allowing remote access while ensuring data security.</p></p>
