<p><p>The indoor humidifier market offers various types of units. Ultrasonic humidifiers use ultrasonic vibrations to create a cool mist. Warm-mist humidifiers produce warm mist through boiling water. Evaporative humidifiers blow moisture into the air using a fan and evaporation process. Vaporizers convert water into steam and release it into the air. Steam to Steam humidifiers generate steam through boiling water with electricity or gas. Each type caters to different needs, providing options for users to choose based on their preferences and requirements for maintaining indoor humidity levels.</p></p>
