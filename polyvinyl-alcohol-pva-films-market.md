<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) Films are classified into two main types: PVA Optical Film and PVA Water Soluble Film. </p><p>PVA Optical Film is used in various applications such as LCD screens, polarized sunglasses, and packaging due to its excellent optical properties and resistance to humidity. </p><p>On the other hand, PVA Water Soluble Film is primarily used in the packaging industry for single-dose laundry detergent packets and agriculture for seed coatings. Its main advantage is its biodegradability and ease of dissolvability in water.</p></p>
