<p><p>Temperature measurement robots are specialized robots equipped with infrared or thermal imaging technology for accurately measuring and monitoring temperatures in various environments. Infrared imaging robots use infrared sensors to detect and measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for non-contact temperature measurements. Thermal imaging robots, on the other hand, use thermal cameras to visualize temperature variations in different objects or surfaces. Both types of robots are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and agriculture for efficient temperature monitoring and control.</p></p>
