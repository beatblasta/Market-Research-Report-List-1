<p><p>The IoT communication module market can be divided into two types: cellular communication modules and non-cellular communication modules. Cellular communication modules utilize cellular networks and technologies like 3G, 4G, and soon 5G to provide wireless connectivity for IoT devices. These modules offer wide coverage and high data transfer rates, making them suitable for applications like smart cities and industrial automation. Non-cellular communication modules encompass various technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, ZigBee, and LoRaWAN, which provide short-range wireless connectivity for IoT devices in areas with existing infrastructure or for low-power applications like smart homes and wearables.</p></p>
