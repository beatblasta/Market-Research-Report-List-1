<p><p>Gun magazines are the devices used to hold and feed firearm ammunition. There are different types of gun magazines available in the market. Tubular magazines are typically found on shotguns and lever-action rifles, where rounds are loaded end to end in a tubular chamber. Detachable box magazines can be easily removed from the firearm to load more rounds. Drum magazines feature a cylindrical shape and can hold a large number of cartridges. Other market types include helical magazines, belt-fed systems, and internal magazines integrated into the firearm's frame or grip. These variations offer different capacities, reload options, and compatibility based on firearm models.</p></p>
