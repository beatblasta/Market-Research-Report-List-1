<p><p>The stationary portable grinder market comprises three types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic grinders are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for tasks requiring high power and increased durability. Electric grinders, on the other hand, operate using electricity and are ideal for smaller tasks that don't require as much power. Lastly, hydraulic grinders use hydraulic pressure to operate and offer high power and precision, making them useful for heavy-duty applications. These various types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the stationary portable grinder market.</p></p>
