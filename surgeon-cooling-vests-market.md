This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of minimally invasive surgeries has led to longer procedure times, exposing surgeons to extended periods of physical exertion and heat stress. Surgeon cooling vests address this issue by providing a cooling effect that helps in maintaining optimal body temperature.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing awareness among healthcare professionals about the importance of surgeon comfort and safety. Surgeon cooling vests not only enhance surgeon performance but also reduce the risk of fatigue and associated medical errors. These vests also help in preventing heat-related illnesses and improving overall surgeon well-being.</p><p>In terms of trends, there has been a shift towards lightweight and flexible cooling vests that offer maximum mobility and comfort.
