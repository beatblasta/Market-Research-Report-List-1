They are tailored to meet the unique requirements of industrial environments, offering specialized features and functions. On the other hand, Ethernet Universal testers provide broader compatibility and flexibility, as they can handle not only Industrial Ethernet protocols but also standard Ethernet protocols commonly found in commercial networks. They offer a more comprehensive solution for testing both industrial and non-industrial Ethernet networks.</p></p>
