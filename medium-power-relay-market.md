<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medium Power Relay market consists of two types: Closed Type and Open Type. In the Closed Type market, the relay has a sealed construction, preventing external factors like dust or moisture from entering the relay, ensuring reliability and durability. In the Open Type market, the relay lacks a sealed construction, making it more accessible and easier to maintain or repair. However, it may be more susceptible to environmental conditions. Both types offer different advantages, thus catering to the diverse needs of customers in the Medium Power Relay market.</p></p>
