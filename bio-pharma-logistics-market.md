<p><p>The bio pharma logistics market can be categorized into two main types: the generic drugs market and the branded drugs market. </p><p>Generic drugs are pharmaceutical products that are bioequivalent to brand-name drugs, meaning they have the same active ingredients, strength, dosage form, and route of administration. They are typically sold at a lower cost and are produced by multiple manufacturers after the patent on the brand-name drug has expired. </p><p>Branded drugs, on the other hand, are marketed under a specific brand name by the company that holds the patent. They are usually more expensive and have exclusive rights for a certain period of time before generic versions become available.</p></p>
