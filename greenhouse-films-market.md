<p><p>Greenhouse Films Market Types - Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE), Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) are widely used in agriculture for crop protection and yield enhancement. EVA films offer high transparency and good resistance to UV radiation, LLDPE films are known for their strength and puncture resistance, while LDPE films are flexible and provide good light transmission. These different types cater to the varying needs of farmers in terms of durability, light transmission, and protection against external factors.</p></p>
