<p><p>The IGBT test system market can be categorized into two types: IGBT Dynamic Test System and IGBT Static Test System. The IGBT Dynamic Test System is used for testing the dynamic performance of IGBTs, including their switching characteristics, voltage and current waveforms, and power dissipation. On the other hand, the IGBT Static Test System is used for evaluating the static electrical parameters of the IGBTs, such as on-state voltage drop, leakage current, and threshold voltage. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning and reliability of IGBT devices.</p></p>
