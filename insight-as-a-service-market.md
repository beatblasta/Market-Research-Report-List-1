<p><p>Insight as a Service (IaaS) is categorized into three market types: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. In the public cloud market, service providers offer IaaS solutions to a wide range of clients via the internet, allowing for scalability and cost-efficiency. Private cloud IaaS is dedicated to a single organization, offering enhanced security and control but requiring higher costs. Hybrid cloud IaaS combines both public and private cloud infrastructure, enabling organizations to utilize the benefits of both while addressing specific needs such as compliance or security concerns.</p></p>
