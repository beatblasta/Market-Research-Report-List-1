<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The steel carports market comprises two main types: vertical roof carports and regular roof carports. Vertical roof carports are designed with vertically oriented roof panels that allow rain, snow, and debris to easily slide off. They have reinforced supports which make them highly durable and suitable for areas with harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, regular roof carports consist of horizontally oriented roof panels and offer basic protection against the elements. They are popular in areas with mild weather and are a more economical option.</p></p>
