<p><p>Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions aim to protect sensitive data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Network DLP focuses on monitoring and securing data flowing across networks, preventing data leaks through email, web applications, or cloud services. Storage DLP focuses on securing data at rest, enforcing encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access to stored data. Endpoint DLP protects data on individual devices like laptops or mobile devices, ensuring that sensitive information is not leaked or stolen via removable media or unauthorized transfers. These solutions help organizations maintain data integrity and comply with regulations.</p></p>
