<p><p>Microsegmentation technology refers to the process of dividing a network into smaller, isolated segments in order to enhance security by controlling and securing data traffic between them. The need for effective protection against advanced threats has also driven the demand for microsegmentation technology.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise of IoT (Internet of Things) devices and the proliferation of mobile devices have further fueled the adoption of microsegmentation technology. As the number of connected devices continues to increase, the need to secure each device and its communication becomes a critical focus for organizations.</p><p>The market growth is also being driven by the escalating adoption of Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and the increasing deployment of virtualization technologies. Microsegmentation technology is a natural fit for these environments, allowing organizations to create isolated segments within virtual networks and apply customized security policies.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing several trends, such as the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning capabilities in microsegmentation solutions to enable real-time threat detection and response.
