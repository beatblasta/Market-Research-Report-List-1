<p><p>Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) tools accessories market consist of three main types of products - drill bits, reamers, and drill pipes. Drill bits are used to cut through the soil and rock during the drilling process, while reamers are used to widen the hole to the desired diameter. Drill pipes are used to connect the drilling rig to the underground drilling tools. These accessories play a crucial role in the success of HDD projects by ensuring efficient and accurate drilling operations.</p></p>
