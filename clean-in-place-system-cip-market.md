<p><p>Clean-in-Place (CIP) systems are automated systems used to clean and sterilize process equipment without disassembly. The market for CIP systems can be divided into two main types: Single-use CIP systems and Reuse CIP systems. Single-use CIP systems are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after each cleaning process, while Reuse CIP systems are designed for multiple uses. Single-use CIP systems provide convenience and eliminate the risk of cross-contamination, while Reuse CIP systems offer cost savings and are more environmentally friendly.</p></p>
