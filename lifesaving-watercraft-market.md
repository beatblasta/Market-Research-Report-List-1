<p><p>The Lifesaving Watercraft market can be categorized into two types: Open Lifesaving Watercraft and Semi-enclosed Lifesaving Watercraft. Open Lifesaving Watercraft refers to watercraft that have an open design, allowing easy access and quick rescue operations. These are typically used in situations where speed and maneuverability are crucial, such as in coastal areas or rapid rivers. On the other hand, Semi-enclosed Lifesaving Watercraft have a partially covered design, providing some protection to passengers. These are suitable for rescue operations in rougher waters or during adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
