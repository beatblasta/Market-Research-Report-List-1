<p><p>The fingerprint safe market comprises two main types: countertop type and embedded type. Countertop fingerprint safes are compact and portable, designed to be placed on a flat surface such as a table or desk. They are commonly used in homes and small offices for storing valuable items securely. On the other hand, embedded fingerprint safes are installed as a built-in feature of larger objects, such as cabinets or drawers, providing a hidden and more permanent solution for securing valuable belongings. These safes are often utilized in commercial settings or high-security environments.</p></p>
