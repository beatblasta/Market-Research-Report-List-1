<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Electromagnetic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cranial Navigation System market comprises various types, including the Optical System and the Electromagnetic System. The Optical System utilizes cameras and markers placed on the patient's head to track their position accurately. It calculates the coordinates of the surgical tools relative to the patient's head. On the other hand, the Electromagnetic System uses sensors attached to the patient's head, which are tracked by a magnetic field emitted by a device. This allows for real-time navigation of surgical instruments, ensuring precise and safe procedures within the cranial region.</p></p>
