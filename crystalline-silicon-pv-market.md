<p><p>The crystalline silicon PV market includes two types of modules: mono-Si modules and multi-Si modules. Mono-Si modules are made from single-crystal silicon, offering high efficiency and better performance in low light conditions, but are more expensive to produce. Multi-Si modules, on the other hand, are made from multiple-crystal silicon, providing a lower efficiency and performance in low light conditions compared to mono-Si modules, but are more cost-effective. Both types are widely used in the solar energy industry, catering to different customer needs and project requirements.</p></p>
