Transactional email is focused on immediate communication, such as order confirmations or password resets. Acquisition email is targeted towards acquiring new customers, often through promotional offers or discounts. Retention email aims to engage and retain existing customers, regular newsletters or personalized content being common examples. Each of these market types serves a specific purpose in mobile email marketing, catering to different stages of customer interaction and nurturing relationships.</p></p>
