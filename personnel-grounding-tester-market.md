<p><p>The Personnel Grounding Tester market consists of two main types - the Wrist Strap Tester and the Combination Tester. On the other hand, Combination Testers are versatile devices that not only check the resistance of wrist straps but also assess the resistance of the grounding system to ensure safety compliance. Both of these testers play a crucial role in maintaining a safe working environment by monitoring personnel grounding in industries and laboratories.</p></p>
