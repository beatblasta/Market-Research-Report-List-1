<p><p>DPC ceramic substrates are essential components in electronic devices due to their excellent thermal conductivity, mechanical strength, and electrical insulation properties. Two popular types of DPC ceramic substrates are alumina and aluminum nitride. Alumina DPC ceramic substrates are widely used in high-power LED modules, power electronic modules, and RF power amplifiers. On the other hand, aluminum nitride DPC ceramic substrates are preferred for their superior thermal conductivity, making them ideal for applications requiring efficient heat dissipation, such as high-frequency communication devices and automotive sensors.</p></p>
