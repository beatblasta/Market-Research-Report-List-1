<p><ul><li>Bone Level Prosthetics</li><li>Tissue Level Prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Implant borne prosthetics refer to dental prosthetics that are supported by implants placed in the jawbone to replace missing teeth. There are two main types in the market: bone level prosthetics and tissue level prosthetics. Bone level prosthetics are placed at the bone level and provide a stable foundation for the prosthetic tooth. Tissue level prosthetics are placed below the gum tissue and are designed to preserve the gum tissue around the prosthetic tooth. Both types aim to provide functional and aesthetic replacement teeth for patients with missing teeth.</p></p>
