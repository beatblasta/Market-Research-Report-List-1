The Western Blotting Analysis Processors market includes various types of systems used for this purpose. Dry blotting systems use a transfer cassette and sponges to transfer proteins from an electrophoresis gel to a membrane. Semi-dry blotting systems use a buffer solution and an electrical current to transfer proteins. Other types of systems may include vacuum blotting or electroblotting, which use different methods to transfer proteins to a membrane.</p></p>
