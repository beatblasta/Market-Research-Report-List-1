<p><p>Breast milk pasteurizers come in two types: water-free pasteurizers and with-water pasteurizers. Water-free pasteurizers utilize heat to kill bacteria in breast milk, without the need for water. These types of pasteurizers are convenient and easy to use, as they do not require any water for operation. On the other hand, with-water pasteurizers involve the use of water to heat the breast milk and eliminate bacteria. These pasteurizers may require a water source and can be slightly more complex to operate. Both types serve the purpose of making breast milk safe for consumption by babies.</p></p>
