<p><p>Pre-Implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) is a procedure used during in vitro fertilization (IVF) to screen embryos for genetic abnormalities before they are implanted in the uterus. There are several market types in PGD. Chromosomal abnormalities testing is done to identify conditions like Down syndrome. X-linked diseases testing detects genetic disorders that mainly affect males. Freeze embryo testing ensures the viability of frozen embryos before transfer. Aneuploidy screening identifies abnormal number of chromosomes. HLA testing is done to match the Human Leukocyte Antigen for organ transplantation. Other types include testing for specific genetic markers or mutations.</p></p>
