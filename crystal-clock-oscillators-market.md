SMD/SMT (Surface Mount Device/Surface Mount Technology) refers to a type of assembly where components are mounted directly on the surface of a PCB. This market type is popular due to its compact size, higher component density, and easy automated assembly. Through Hole, on the other hand, involves mounting components by inserting their leads into holes on the PCB and soldering them on the other side. This market type is preferred for its robustness and ease of manual assembly.</p></p>
