<p><strong>The Airport Weather Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Airport Weather Station</li><li>Portable Airport Weather Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport weather station market can be divided into two main types: embedded airport weather stations and portable airport weather stations. </p><p>Embedded airport weather stations are permanently installed at the airport and provide continuous accurate weather information for air traffic control and airport operations. They are typically installed on a fixed structure like a mast or a tower.</p><p>On the other hand, portable airport weather stations are designed to be easily transportable and can be deployed in different locations as needed. They are often used for temporary or remote airfields and provide essential weather data for flight operations in those areas.</p></p>
