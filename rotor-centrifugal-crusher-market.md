<p><p>The Rotor Centrifugal Crusher Market consists of three types: Rock on Rock, Rock on Iron, and Rock on Anvil. In the Rock on Rock type, the rotor and crushing chamber are aligned in such a way that the rock material is crushed against itself. In the Rock on Iron type, the rotor and crushing chamber are designed to crush rocks against an iron surface. In the Rock on Anvil type, the rotor and crushing chamber utilize an anvil to crush the rock material.</p></p>
