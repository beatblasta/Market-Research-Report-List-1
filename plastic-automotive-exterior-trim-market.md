<p><p>The plastic automotive exterior trim market is comprised of various types, including PP (Polypropylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PE (Polyethylene), ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene), and other materials. PP is known for its high strength, impact resistance, and flexibility, making it suitable for bumper fascias and rocker panels. PVC offers excellent weather resistance and is commonly used for window seals and body side moldings. PE is lightweight, durable, and used for weatherstrips and wheel arch liners. ABS combines strength with heat resistance and is utilized for grilles and exterior trims. Other materials may include composites or specialty polymers with unique properties.</p></p>
