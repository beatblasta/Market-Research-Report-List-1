<p><p>Thulium foil is a thin sheet made from the rare earth metal thulium. Thulium is a silver-gray metal that is highly malleable and ductile, allowing it to be easily pressed into thin foils. These properties make it suitable for various applications in industries like aerospace, electronics, energy, and medical devices.</p><p>The global thulium foil market is expected to witness steady growth during the forecast period. Thulium foil is also extensively used in electronics for applications such as semiconductors, display devices, and sensors, as it offers high electrical conductivity and thermal stability.</p><p>Moreover, the growing demand for renewable energy sources is driving the use of thulium foil in the energy sector.
