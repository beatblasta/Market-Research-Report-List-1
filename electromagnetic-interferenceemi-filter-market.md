<p><p>An Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) filter, also known as a noise filter or RF filter, is a device used to suppress electromagnetic interference and radio frequency interference. It is designed to block unwanted signals or noise that can disrupt the performance of electronic devices. EMI filters typically consist of passive components such as inductors, capacitors, and resistors, which are arranged in various configurations to attenuate electromagnetic interference.</p><p>The global EMI filter market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing use of electronic devices in industries such as automotive, healthcare, telecommunications, and aerospace is driving the demand for EMI filters.
