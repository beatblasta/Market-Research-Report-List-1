<p><p>A full-face gas mask is a personal protective equipment (PPE) device that covers the entire face and provides respiratory protection against various types of hazardous gases, chemicals, and airborne particles. Full-face gas masks are commonly used in industrial settings, firefighting, military applications, and emergency response situations.</p><p>The global full-face gas mask market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. The market analysis indicates that factors such as increasing awareness about workplace safety, stringent regulations regarding worker protection, and the growing demand from defense and law enforcement organizations are driving the market growth.
