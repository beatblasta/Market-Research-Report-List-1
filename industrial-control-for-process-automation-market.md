<p><p>Industrial control for process automation is a market that encompasses various types of control systems used in industries. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems monitor and control multiple remote sites. Distributed Control Systems (DCS) focus on controlling complex processes across multiple locations. Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software manages a product's lifecycle from design to disposal. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) automate machinery and processes. Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) gather data from remote locations. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) manage and optimize production processes. Human-Machine Interfaces (HMIs) provide a user-friendly interface for operators to control and monitor industrial processes.</p></p>
