<p><p>The forklift monitoring system market includes various types such as speed monitoring, overload protection, efficiency monitoring, and security monitoring. Speed monitoring refers to monitoring the speed of forklifts to prevent accidents and maintain safety. Overload protection involves monitoring the weight being carried by forklifts to avoid excessive strain and potential accidents. Efficiency monitoring focuses on monitoring fuel consumption and productivity of forklifts to optimize operations. Security monitoring involves monitoring the location and usage of forklifts to prevent theft and unauthorized access.</p></p>
