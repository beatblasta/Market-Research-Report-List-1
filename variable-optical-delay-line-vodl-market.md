There are two main market types for VODL: manual and motorized. The manual VODL requires manual adjustments to change the delay, making it suitable for applications with static or infrequent delay requirements. On the other hand, the motorized VODL can be remotely controlled and offers precise and dynamic delay adjustments, making it ideal for applications with frequent or real-time delay variations.</p></p>
