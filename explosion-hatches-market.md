<p><ul><li>Weight Type</li><li>Oil Seal Type</li><li>Spring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Explosion hatches are designed to safely release pressure during explosions or pressure buildup in enclosed spaces. The Weight Type explosion hatches rely on a weighted lever mechanism to open and allow the pressure to escape. Oil Seal Type hatches utilize an oil seal system that breaks when pressure exceeds a certain limit. Spring Type hatches employ a spring-loaded mechanism to open and release pressure. These different types cater to varying industrial needs, providing diverse options for ensuring safety during potential explosive incidents.</p></p>
