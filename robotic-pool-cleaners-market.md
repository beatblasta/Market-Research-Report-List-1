<p><p>Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive are two types of robotic pool cleaners available in the market. They are equipped with rubber tracks that help them climb walls and stairs. On the other hand, Wheel Drive cleaners feature wheels that allow them to easily navigate through the pool.
