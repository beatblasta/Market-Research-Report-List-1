<p><ul><li>Cone Plate Rheometer</li><li>Concentric Cylinder Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dynamic Shear Rheometer market consists of two types of rheometers: Cone Plate Rheometer and Concentric Cylinder Rheometer. Cone Plate Rheometer measures the viscosity of fluids by placing a cone-shaped tool on a fixed plate. This type is commonly used for evaluating liquid-like materials such as coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, the Concentric Cylinder Rheometer measures the viscosity of materials by rotating an inner cylinder within an outer cylinder. This type is mostly used for studying solid-like materials, such as polymers and gels.</p></p>
