<p><strong>The Commercial Airships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Semi Rigid</li><li>Non-rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial airships can be categorized into three main types based on their structure: rigid, semi-rigid, and non-rigid. Rigid airships have a framework structure that maintains their shape and allows for greater stability and size. Semi-rigid airships have a partially rigid structure, combining the benefits of stability and flexibility. Non-rigid airships, also known as blimps, have no internal structure and rely on the pressure of the lifting gas to maintain their shape. Each market type offers unique advantages and is used for different purposes in the commercial airship industry.</p></p>
