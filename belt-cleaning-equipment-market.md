<p><p>Belt cleaning equipment is used to remove debris, dust, and residue from conveyor belts to ensure efficient operation in various industries. Two commonly used types of belt cleaning equipment are Scrapers and Brushes. Scrapers consist of a blade that scrapes off material from the belt's surface, while Brushes use rotating bristles to clean the belt. Both types are designed to enhance belt performance by minimizing belt slippage, reducing material carryback, and preventing belt damage. Their effectiveness and suitability depend on factors such as belt material, application, and specific cleaning requirements.</p></p>
