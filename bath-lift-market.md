<p><p>The bath lift market consists of two main types: automatic bath lifts and semi-auto bath lifts. On the other hand, semi-auto bath lifts require some manual effort from the user, usually in the form of a lever or button, to lower and raise themselves in the bath. Both types aim to provide individuals with a safe and comfortable bathing experience, catering to different levels of assistance required by users.</p></p>
