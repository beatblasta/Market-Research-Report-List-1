<p><p>The Coronavirus Disease 2019 Test Kit market can be divided into two types: Nucleic Acid Test Kit and Antibody Test Kit. The Nucleic Acid Test Kit detects the presence of the virus by analyzing genetic material from a patient's sample. It is highly accurate but requires specialized laboratory equipment. On the other hand, the Antibody Test Kit identifies antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus. This type of test is faster and easier to perform, but it may have lower sensitivity in the early stages of infection. Both test kits are crucial in combating the COVID-19 pandemic.</p></p>
