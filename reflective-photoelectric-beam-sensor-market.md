<p><p>A reflective photoelectric beam sensor is a type of sensor that utilizes a beam of light to detect the presence or absence of an object. It consists of an emitter and a receiver, with the receiver detecting the light emitted by the emitter. When an object obstructs the beam, the receiver registers the change in the received light intensity, indicating the presence of the object.</p><p>The reflective photoelectric beam sensor market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue to do so in the coming years.
