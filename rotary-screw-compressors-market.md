<p><p>Rotary screw compressors are a type of gas compressor that works on the principle of rotary displacement. These compressors are widely used in various industries due to their high efficiency, reliability, and low maintenance requirements.</p><p>The global rotary screw compressors market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. The increasing demand for energy-efficient compressors in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and oil & gas has been a major driver for market growth. <p><p>Rotary screw compressors are categorized into two types based on the type of compression mechanism they employ. Oil-free screw compressors operate without any oil lubrication, ensuring clean and contaminant-free compressed air. They are widely used in applications where air quality is crucial, such as food processing and electronics manufacturing. On the other hand, oil-injected screw compressors use oil for lubrication, resulting in a higher efficiency and lifespan. This makes them suitable for various industrial applications, including automotive and construction sectors.</p></p>
