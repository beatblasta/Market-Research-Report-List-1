<p><p>MIG welding torches are available in two main types: water cooled and air cooled. Water cooled torches use a cooling mechanism that involves circulating water around the torch, helping to dissipate heat and increase its lifespan during prolonged welding operations. On the other hand, air-cooled torches rely on the surrounding air to cool down, which makes them more lightweight and easier to maneuver. The choice between the two types depends on the intensity and duration of welding required, as well as personal preferences and specific welding applications.</p></p>
