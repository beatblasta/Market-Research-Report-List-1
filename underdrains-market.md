<p><p>Underdrains are drainage systems commonly used in construction projects to remove excess water from the soil. There are several types of underdrains available in the market. Flat underdrains are thin and broad, providing uniform water distribution and easy maintenance. Filter cover underdrains have a protective layer that prevents soil clogging and ensures efficient water flow. Cone underdrains are shaped like cones, optimizing drainage capacity in areas with high water flow. Horizontal culvert underdrains are placed horizontally to collect water and redirect it to drainage outlets. These different types cater to varying soil and water flow conditions in construction projects.</p></p>
