<p><p>The optical lens polisher market consists of two types: automatic and manual. Automatic lens polishers are machines that use advanced technology to automatically polish lenses, increasing efficiency and reducing human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume polishing operations and offer features like multiple polishing heads and programmable settings. On the other hand, manual lens polishers require human operation and are more suitable for low-volume polishing needs or situations where customization is required. These lens polishers typically have simpler designs and are manually controlled by the operator.</p></p>
