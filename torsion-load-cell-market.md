<p><p>The Torsion Load Cell market can be categorized into two types: Non-Contact Torsion Load Cell and Strain Gauge Torsion Load Cell. Non-Contact Torsion Load Cell utilizes non-contact measurement techniques such as magnetic or optical methods to measure torque or torsion. On the other hand, Strain Gauge Torsion Load Cell measures torque by using the principle of strain gauges that are affixed to a flexible element, which deforms when subjected to torsion. Both types play a crucial role in torque measurement, providing accurate and reliable results for a variety of applications.</p></p>
