These include insulated gate bipolar transistor (IGBT), metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistor (MOSFET), diode, intelligent power module (IPM), and silicon/silicon carbide (Si/SiC) hybrid module. IGBT is widely used in industrial applications for its ability to handle high voltages and currents. MOSFETs are commonly used in low voltage applications due to their high efficiency. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction and are essential for rectification. IPMs integrate multiple power devices into one module for improved performance and efficiency. Si/SiC hybrid modules combine the benefits of both silicon and silicon carbide materials, enabling higher power density and better thermal management.</p></p>
