<p><p>The laparoscopic retrieval bag market consists of two types: manual opening and automatic opening bags. Manual opening bags require the surgeon to manually open the bag after it is inserted into the abdominal cavity to hold and remove tissues or organs during laparoscopic procedures. On the other hand, automatic opening bags are designed to automatically open once deployed inside the body, reducing the need for manual manipulation. Both types serve the same purpose of safely retrieving specimens and minimizing contamination risks during laparoscopic surgeries.</p></p>
