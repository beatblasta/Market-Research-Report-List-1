AI-powered imaging solutions can assist radiologists in analyzing and interpreting images, thus reducing the chances of human errors and improving diagnostic accuracy.</p><p>Overall, the orthopedic medical imaging market is set to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by the high prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders and advancements in imaging technology.
