<p><p>Stretch film machinery refers to the equipment used for wrapping products with stretch film to provide protection during transportation and storage. The market for this machinery is categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machinery performs the entire wrapping process automatically, including film feeding, cutting, and heat sealing. On the other hand, semi-automatic machinery requires some manual intervention, such as manually placing the product on the turntable and initiating the wrapping process. Both types of machinery offer efficient and dependable wrapping solutions for various industries.</p></p>
