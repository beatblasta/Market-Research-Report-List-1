<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Extended wear contact lenses are available in three different market types: spherical form, toric form, and multifocal form. </p><p>Spherical form lenses are designed to correct common vision problems like nearsightedness and farsightedness. They are the most commonly used lenses and provide clear vision at all distances.</p><p>Toric form lenses are specifically designed for individuals with astigmatism. These lenses have different powers in different meridians of the lens to correct the irregular shape of the cornea and provide clear, stable vision.</p><p>Multifocal form lenses are used to correct presbyopia, a condition that occurs with age and affects the ability to focus on near objects. These lenses have multiple lens powers to allow for clear vision at various distances.</p><p>Overall, extended wear contact lenses offer a wide range of options to meet the specific vision needs of individuals.</p></p>
