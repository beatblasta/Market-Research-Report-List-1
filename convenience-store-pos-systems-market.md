<p><p>Convenience store POS systems are available in two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based POS systems store data on remote servers accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. They require minimal upfront costs and provide automatic updates and backups. On the other hand, on-premises POS systems store data locally on the store's own servers. They offer complete control over data and security, but require higher upfront costs, maintenance, and backups. Both market types cater to the specific needs and preferences of convenience store owners.</p></p>
