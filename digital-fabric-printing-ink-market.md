<p><p>Digital fabric printing ink market is segmented into various types including Reactive Dye Inks, Acidic Ink, Paint Ink, Dispersion Ink, and Sublimation Ink. Reactive Dye Inks are widely used for their vivid colors and durability. Acidic Inks are suitable for printing on synthetic fibers. Paint Inks offer a wider color gamut and strong adhesion. Dispersion Inks are preferred for their fast drying time. Sublimation Inks are commonly used for printing on polyester fabrics due to their ability to sublimate from solid to gas without passing through a liquid state.</p></p>
