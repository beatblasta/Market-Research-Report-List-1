<p><p>Rotary isolators are electrical switches used to disconnect power supply from the load. The single pole rotary isolators have only one pole, providing power control to a single circuit. They are commonly used in residential and small commercial settings. Multi-pole rotary isolators, on the other hand, have multiple poles and are utilized in industrial applications where controlling power to several circuits is necessary. These isolators are important for ensuring safety during maintenance and emergency situations, allowing easy disconnection from power sources.</p></p>
