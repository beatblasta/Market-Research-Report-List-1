<p><p>The Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter (GFCI) market includes two primary types: Receptacle-Type GFCI and Portable GFCI. Receptacle-Type GFCI is a device installed directly into wall outlets to provide protection against electrical shocks. It automatically interrupts the circuit if it detects a fault or imbalance in the electric current. On the other hand, Portable GFCI is a compact, standalone device that can be plugged into an outlet and used with various electrical appliances. It offers protection against ground faults and is commonly used in construction sites or outdoor settings.</p></p>
