<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Ceiling air conditioners can be categorized based on the number of air outlets they have. Single out wind models have one air outlet, typically found in the center of the unit, to distribute cool air across the room. Two out wind models have two air outlets positioned on opposite sides of the unit, providing air circulation in two directions. Four out wind models feature four air outlets, often located at each corner of the unit, maximizing air distribution within the space. These various types cater to different room layouts and cooling needs.</p></p>
