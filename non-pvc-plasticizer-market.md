<p><p>Non-PVC plasticizers are additives used in plastic materials to improve flexibility and durability. Different types of non-PVC plasticizers include phthalates, trimellitates, aliphatic, and benzoates. Phthalates are commonly used but are facing restrictions due to potential health risks. Trimellitates are considered as safer alternatives, while aliphatic and benzoates are also gaining popularity for their low toxicity and environmental impact. These different types of plasticizers cater to the varying preferences and requirements of the market, ensuring a diverse range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
