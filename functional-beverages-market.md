Functional beverages are perceived as a more convenient and accessible way to improve health and wellbeing compared to other dietary changes. Moreover, rising health consciousness and increasing disposable incomes are driving the demand for functional beverages, especially in emerging economies.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing various trends that are shaping its growth. One of the key trends is the increasing popularity of natural and organic functional beverages. Consumers are becoming more aware of the potential health risks associated with artificial ingredients and are demanding products that are free from additives, preservatives, and synthetic substances. As a result, manufacturers are responding to these demands by introducing natural and organic functional beverages into the market.</p><p>Another trend in the functional beverages market is the focus on specific health benefits. Manufacturers are developing new products that cater to these specific needs, providing consumers with a wide range of options to choose from.</p><p>In conclusion, the functional beverages market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
