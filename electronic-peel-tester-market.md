<p><p>An electronic peel tester is an instrument used to measure the strength of adhesive bonds by examining their peel resistance. It securely attaches a flexible adherent strip to a rigid substrate, then gradually peels the strip from the substrate at a defined angle and speed until it is completely detached. The force required to separate the two surfaces is measured and used to determine the peel strength of the adhesive bond.</p><p>According to the market growth analysis, the electronic peel tester market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The Electronic Peel Tester market consists of four main types: Motor, Hydraulic, Pneumatic, and Electromagnetic market. In the Motor market, the tester operates using a motor-driven mechanism to perform peel tests. The Hydraulic market involves the use of hydraulic systems to generate the force required for the tests. Pneumatic testers utilize compressed air or gas to generate the necessary force. Lastly, Electromagnetic testers utilize electromagnetic induction to generate force for conducting peel tests. These various market types provide different options and capabilities for conducting efficient and accurate peel tests on electronic components.</p></p>
