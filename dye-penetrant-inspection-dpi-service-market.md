<p><p>Dye Penetrant Inspection (DPI) Service is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-breaking defects in materials such as metals, plastics, ceramics, and composites. This technique is widely employed in various industries including aerospace, automotive, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p><p>During a DPI test, a liquid dye is applied to the surface of the material being examined. The dye is left on the surface for a specific period, allowing it to seep into any defects present. After the dye is removed, a developer is applied to draw out the dye from the defects, making them visible. This enables technicians to identify any cracks, fractures, or other defects that may affect the quality or safety of the material.</p><p>The global Dye Penetrant Inspection (DPI) Service market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
