<p><p>The induced draft fan market comprises three main types: axial, radial, and centrifugal. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan shaft, making them suitable for applications requiring high airflow volumes and low pressures. Radial fans, on the other hand, move air outward perpendicularly to the fan shaft, enabling them to generate greater pressures. Centrifugal fans combine features of both axial and radial fans, allowing them to provide higher pressures and volumes. These fan types cater to various industries and their specific needs for ventilation, cooling, and exhaust purposes.</p></p>
