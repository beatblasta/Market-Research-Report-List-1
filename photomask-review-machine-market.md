<p><strong>The Photomask Review Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review Machine market consists of two types: the Die to Die (DD) Method and the Die to Database (DB) Method. In the DD method, the machine compares two photomasks of the same die to identify any defects or inconsistencies between them. On the other hand, the DB method compares the photomask with the database design to ensure accuracy during the manufacturing process. Both methods play a crucial role in detecting errors and improving the quality of photomasks used in semiconductor production.</p></p>
