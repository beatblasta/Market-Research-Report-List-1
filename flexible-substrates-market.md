The three main types of flexible substrates are plastic, glass, and metal. Plastic substrates are lightweight and cost-effective, making them popular for applications like flexible displays and wearable electronics. Glass substrates offer higher transparency and durability, making them suitable for use in touchscreens and solar cells. Metal substrates provide excellent thermal conductivity and structural support, making them ideal for applications like RFID tags and smart cards.</p></p>
