<p><p>Polyurethane thermally conductive adhesive refers to a type of adhesive that is designed to have good thermal conductivity properties. The market for such adhesives includes two major types: polyurethane thermally conductive potting compound and polyurethane thermally conductive gap filler. Potting compounds are used to encapsulate electronic components, providing insulation and protection while also facilitating heat transfer. Gap fillers, on the other hand, are used to fill voids or gaps between heat-generating components and heat sinks, enhancing thermal conduction. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper heat dissipation in electronic devices.</p></p>
