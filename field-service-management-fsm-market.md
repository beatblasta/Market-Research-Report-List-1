<p><p>Field Service Management (FSM) is a system that helps businesses manage and optimize their field service operations. The market for FSM can be divided into two types: On-premises FSM and Cloud FSM. On-premises FSM is a software solution that is locally installed and hosted by the business itself, providing greater control and security but with higher upfront costs. Cloud FSM, on the other hand, is web-based software hosted on the cloud, offering greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as businesses can access and manage their field service operations from any device with an internet connection.</p></p>
