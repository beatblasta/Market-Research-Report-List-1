<p><p>A PVD coated faucet is a type of faucet that is coated using a Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) process. The PVD coating enhances the appearance and durability of the faucet by providing it with a protective layer that is resistant to scratches, tarnishing, and other forms of wear and tear.</p><p>The PVD coated faucet market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years.
