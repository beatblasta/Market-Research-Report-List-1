<p><p>The Universal Head Mounted Temperature Transmitters market includes three types: 2-Wire, 3-Wire, and 4-Wire. A 2-Wire transmitter is a simple design, where both the power supply and the signal are transmitted through the same pair of wires. The 3-Wire transmitter consists of three wires, where two wires transmit power and one wire transmits the signal. The 4-Wire transmitter, on the other hand, uses separate pairs of wires for power supply and for transmitting the signal. These different types allow for flexibility in wiring configurations and meet various industry requirements.</p></p>
