<p><p>Roller ball pens are divided into two main market types: telescopic and retractable. Telescopic roller ball pens have a cap that can be removed to reveal the pen tip, ensuring protection when not in use. They are lightweight and easy to carry around. On the other hand, retractable roller ball pens have a mechanism that allows the tip to retract into the pen body, eliminating the need for a cap. This design offers convenience and quick access to writing. Both of these market types cater to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
