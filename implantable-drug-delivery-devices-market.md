<p><p>The implantable drug delivery devices market can be categorized into two types: titanium drug delivery systems and plastic drug delivery systems. Titanium drug delivery systems are made of titanium material and are suitable for long-term drug delivery applications. They provide stability, durability, and biocompatibility. On the other hand, plastic drug delivery systems are made of plastic materials and are commonly used for short-term drug delivery applications. They are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer flexibility in terms of device design and drug release mechanisms.</p></p>
